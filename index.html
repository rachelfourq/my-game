How To Play

This is a TWO Player game, in which a word will automatically be generated for you...only it will be in the WRONG order. Your task is to drag and drop the letters into the correct order. You will have 6 seconds to solve. If you solve, you get another turn! If not, the turn will move to the next player

Click the "Let's Play" button to begin.
